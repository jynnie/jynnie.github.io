The Summoner

A party game where players must decode the spell to banish a demon, but traitors hide in their midst. Inspired by mafia-like games and collaborative puzzles, the Summoner is a party game solo developed in August 2018. Currently, the game is being playtested and balanced. Look forward to future updates.

intro.

Your party comprises an order of guardians vowed to protect the ancient magics. However, your party discovers one of you has betrayed the order and begun to summon a demon. Before the demon is summoned, figure out what combination of magic will vanquish the summoning circle. Or if you are a traitor, fulfill your sinister plans...
